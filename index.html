About Me

Hello! I'm Niranjan Sujay, a robotics enthusiast and researcher specializing in robotic perception, AI, and autonomous systems. I am passionate about integrating AI with robotics to create innovative solutions.

Currently, I am a PhD student and a Robotic Research Intern, working on various research projects such as UrbanNav and Curb2Door. My goal is to contribute to the development of autonomous systems that can navigate complex environments with precision.

Projects

UrbanNav Project

This project uses the Unitree Go1 robot dog with Insta360 camera, Livox LiDAR, and GPS for navigation. The goal is to improve autonomous navigation through training models based on collected data.

Curb2Door Project

Utilizing a mount with Livox, Insta360 camera, and u-blox GPS, this project focuses on reconstructing 3D point clouds for urban mapping and navigation purposes.
