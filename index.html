My research focuses on advancing robotic perception, mapping, and autonomy, with a special interest in sensor fusion, state estimation, and localization for robotic systems. I am currently working on multiple projects that integrate cutting-edge techniques in AI and machine learning to push the boundaries of autonomous systems.

Explore My Projects

Click on the project names below to explore more details:

  • UrbanNav - Autonomous navigation using a Unitree Go1 robot dog equipped with various sensors.
  • SwimmerBot - A robotic swimmer tracker for precise athlete tracking and video recording in aquatic environments.
  • MappingNYC - A project involving advanced LiDAR and GPS systems for detailed urban mapping.
  • Curb2Door - Environment reconstruction using LiDAR, camera, and GPS data for efficient mapping and localization.
  • Diffusion Models - Research on using diffusion models for trajectory prediction and motion forecasting in autonomous systems.
  • Biped Robot - Development of a biped robot with advanced walking algorithms and dynamic stability control.
